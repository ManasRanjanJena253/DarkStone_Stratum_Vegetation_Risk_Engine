RISK CLASSIFICATION

Understanding Hazard Levels

HIGH RISK

Powerlines within 300m of high-density forest. Immediate wildfire spread potential. Requires urgent mitigation.

MEDIUM RISK

Powerlines within 600m of medium-density forest. Elevated wildfire risk under dry conditions. Plan maintenance.

LOW RISK

Powerlines within 1km of sparse forest. Monitor conditions. Low immediate threat but worth tracking.
